Program Overview
As part of the State of California’s Small Business Access to Capital Program, Nor-Cal FDC is committed to helping businesses acquire the capital they need. Our program is specifically designed for businesses that have won or have been awarded a State Contract.
Your State of California awarded contract makes you eligible for the California Certified Business Financing Program. Whether you need working capital, equipment leasing, a line of credit or capital for supplies or materials Nor-Cal FDC and its Lending Partners have a wide range of financial solutions to quickly acquire the capital you need.
